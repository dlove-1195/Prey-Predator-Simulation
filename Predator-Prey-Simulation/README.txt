-Predators chase prey when detected, preys try to avoid predators when deteced, otherwise wander randomly. The chase and flee reaction is also shown in the debug log.
-Predators have vision of filed of cone of about 45 degrees along its moving direction with a certain radius, preys have omniscience with a certain radius.
-When a prey is reached by a predator, it will disappear and be regenerated at a random spot.
-Additional features: Preys and predators cannot go across the cube obstacles and the walls. Can control the numbers of prey and predator at the right sidebar when clicking the ManageAgent class.
